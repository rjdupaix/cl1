Upcoming Titles

The Hunger of the God's

John Gwynne will be giving us the next installment of the norse fantasy series early this year. We last left off in a state where things were coming to a head and out three main character's paths were about to cross. I'm excited to see where he takes us from here.

Release Date: April 12, 2022

The Lost Metal

We have been waiting for 'The Lost Metal' for a long time now. It feels like we have been left on the cliff hanger from the last book for years now, which we have. It will be very interesting to see how Brandon Sanderson can wrap this all up and give us a satisfactory ending, but as we all know, it is a mistake to doubt in this man.

November 15, 2022

The Doors of Stone

Will Patrick Rothfus ever get the gonads to finally release the third title of the Kingkiller Chronicles? We aren't sure, but as of late it seems so. He gave us a reading of the first chapter of 'The Doors of Stone' on Youtube a few weeks ago and it seems to have fans excited. There is no estimated release date, but we can be hopeful that it Will happen either this year or next.

Release Date: Not sure
